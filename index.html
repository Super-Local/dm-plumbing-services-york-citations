What should I do in a plumbing emergency at home?

If you have a burst pipe, major leak, or sudden loss of water pressure, the first step is to turn off your stopcock — usually found under the kitchen sink or where the water supply enters the property. This limits damage while you arrange help. Call a local plumber in Haxby or York as quickly as possible, and if there is any risk of water near electrics, turn off your power at the fuse board too.
